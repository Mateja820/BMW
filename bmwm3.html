BMW M3

  • by:BMW COMPANY
  • date:March 17.3.2020 8:00 am
  • 3 Comments

The BMW M3 is a high-performance version of the Series 3, developed by BMW's Motorsport division BMW M GmbH . The M3 models are derived from the respective generations of the BMW 3 Series. The initial model was available in a coupe style body. Sometimes the M3 is also available in sedan and convertible styles . Due to coupe and convertible models that are no longer part of the 2015 Series 3, the [1] [2] F82 / F83 coupe and convertible models are now called the M4 based on the newly introduced Series 4. The name M3 remains in use only for the sedan version. Upgrades to standard 3 Series cars include more powerful and faster engines, improved steering, suspension and braking systems, aerodynamic body enhancements, lightweight components, and interior and exterior accents with a three-color "M" (motorsport) emblem.
